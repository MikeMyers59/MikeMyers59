Combat & Weapons Systems

Combat & Weapons Systems Directorate produces Non-Propulsion Electronic Systems (NPES) for US Navy nuclear submarines. Leveraging the latest in state-of-the-art technologies, NPES systems are provided for all submarine classes:

  • Sonar
  • Combat Control
  • Ship Control
  • Total Ship Monitoring
  • Tactical and Non-Tactical Data Processing
  • Exterior Communications
  • Navigation
  • ESM
  • Radar
  • Imaging
  • Periscope
  • Weapon Launch Control
  • Weapons Shipping and Handling



Our customers are the men and women who serve this great country, they are the best in the world and expect the best from us. Our mission is to transform challenging concepts into reality in the development of complex submarine systems. Developing and delivering product packages external to the submarine is a dangerous business. Our work is built on solid system engineering practices and foundations, delivering on time and within budget, while maintaining a high level of quality.

Our systems must work ... every time ...without exception.

The Crew, The Navy, and Our Nation depend on us.

Our successes have made us the premier developer and integrator of our product systems, the best in the world in tactical submarine systems. This is only possible by the technical excellence and hard work of the Combat & Weapons Systems Team and Electric Boat Employees.
